My Favourite Movies

Here is a list of my all time favourite movies for Bollywood lovers!

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

It is drama full of comedy, romance and friendship. Also, it has the best actors :p

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

This movie defines the term YOLO and we must not waste any chance in living our lives to the fullest. We should cherish each and every moment and not dwelve on our past mistakes.

Jab We Met

It is a family drama which teaches self love. And if you are looking for a walking green flag, meet Aditya in this movie. You will love him. We all do.
